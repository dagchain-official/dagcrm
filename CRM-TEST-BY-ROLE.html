★ Cross-Role Flow — Ek lead sab roles ke through

Real business jaise — ek lead alag-alag logins se travel karta hai. Har login karke ye dekho:

🎯 Lead → Paisa (poora cycle, role-by-role)

  1. admin Integration Hub → platform Connect → Send Test Lead (ya Leads → Import)
  2. smgr Leads → Distribute → lead RM ko assign + RM notify
  3. rm.b My Workspace me lead dikhe → call log → status qualified
  4. rm.b Opportunity banao → stage won
  5. rm.b Customer 360 → Add Revenue
  6. tl Team Dashboard me rm.b ki performance dikhe (monitor)
  7. finance Finance Dashboard me revenue + profit update
  8. admin Incentives → Auto-Calculate → RM ko incentive → Payroll me flow
  9. hr Payroll me incentive included → Payslip PDF

→ 6 alag logins, ek lead — sab connected. Har role apna part dekhe, sirf utna jitna uska kaam.

🏖️ Leave cycle (3 roles)

  1. rm.b My Leaves → Apply
  2. tl 🔔 notification → Leaves → Approve (sirf apni team)
  3. rm.b 🔔 "approved" → status updated

🔐 Permission change (live, 2 roles)

  1. admin Permission Matrix → Sales Manager → Revenue "Edit" ON
  2. smgr Revenue page → ab Edit button aa gaya
