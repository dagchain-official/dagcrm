# What We Need From You — DAGOS (Simple Version)

Hi,

To move ahead and connect all your businesses into DAGOS, please share the things below. You can send them one by one — no need to wait for everything. Share passwords/keys with us safely (we'll give you a secure link).

---

## 1. Connection to each business's software

We want your sales and revenue numbers to come into DAGOS on their own, instead of typing by hand. For that, we need a way to "connect" to each business's software.

For **every** business, just tell us:
- **Which software do you use?** (name of the tool)
- **The login or access key** for it
- **Who manages it** (a contact person)

**For each business specifically, we want these numbers to flow in:**

- **FX Artha (Forex)** — deposits, withdrawals, lots traded, brokerage, and money held per client.
  *(Most important: a connection that sends us deposit/withdraw and brokerage events automatically.)*
- **DAGChain** — node sales, storage sales, DGCC coin sales, renewals.
- **DAGGPT** — subscriptions, API usage, enterprise deals, active users.
- **DAGDB** — storage used, contracts, accounts.
- **Energy DAO** — investments/participation, and payouts.
- **DAG Army** — course sales, students, certifications.

👉 If any business has **no software** and you use Excel sheets, just tell us — we'll set it up for manual entry.

---

## 2. For pulling leads from ads

- **Facebook:** App ID, App Secret, Page ID, and access to your Lead Ad forms.
- **Google:** Google Ads access (the keys + your account ID).

(This lets new leads from your ads drop straight into DAGOS.)

---

## 3. Documents and logos

- **Sample formats** for: Proposal, Quotation, Invoice, and Finance/Receipt papers — so we make them look exactly like yours.
- **Logo files** for each business and the main brand (good quality, clear background).

---

## 4. Email

- The email address DAGOS should send mails from, and its login details (so the system can send proposals, alerts, etc.).

---

## 5. Product list with prices

- A full list of products for each business, with the current **price** and whether it's **one-time, monthly, or per-unit**.

---

## 6. Your rules (targets & incentives)

Since DAGOS lets **you** control the rules, just tell us your current ones so we set them up first:
- How you decide a person's **target**.
- Your **incentive** rules (who gets what reward, and when).
- For Forex: how you calculate **Net New AUM** and a client's **contribution**.

---

## 7. Slab Incentive details (please write this clearly)

For the slab (tier-based) incentives, please give us a clear document with:
- All **products** it applies to
- The **slabs** (the ranges/levels)
- The **percentage or amount** for each slab
- All the **conditions and special rules**
- Any **exceptions** or unusual cases

👉 **Let's also do one separate call only about the Slab Logic**, so we go through every condition once before we build it. This makes sure it's built exactly the way you want.

---

Once we have these, we'll start connecting your businesses one at a time — beginning with whichever one you want live first. Happy to hop on a quick call for anything above.

Thanks!
