3-Layer Access Control — System ka dimaag

Sabse important concept. Har user ko kya dikhega aur kya nahi — ye 3 layers decide karte hain. Pehle ye samjho, baaki sab samajh aa jayega.

1Role-Based Access

Har role ka apna dashboard + sidebar me sirf uske modules. HR ko HR dashboard, Sales ko sales dashboard.

Why? Login karte hi banda apna kaam dekhe — 25 modules me se dhoondhna na pade. HR ko leads ka distribution nahi chahiye, Sales ko payroll nahi.

2Business Access (Permission Matrix)

Ek RM ko sirf kuch businesses ka data (Forex, DAGChain) — baaki (Energy DAO) ka bilkul nahi. Admin Permission Matrix se control karta hai.

Why? Bade group me alag-alag businesses hote hain. RM A sirf Forex sambhalta hai to use Energy DAO ki leads/revenue kyun dikhe? Confidentiality + focus.

3Module CRUD Permissions

Sirf dekhna ya edit bhi? Sales Manager Revenue dekh sakta hai par edit nahi. Finance dono kar sakta hai. Har role × module × (View/Create/Edit/Delete).

Why? Role kaafi nahi. Manager ko revenue dekhna hai par galti se badalna nahi chahiye. Finance hi paisa edit kare. Ye fine-grained control deta hai.

Leads + Distribution + CSV Import

Lead = potential customer. Sabse important module — sales yahin se shuru hota hai.

#StepExpected
3.1Sidebar → Leads → + New → bharo → SaveToast "Leads created", list me aaye
3.2Upar Filter dropdowns (Status, Source) use karoList filter ho jaye
3.3Score column dekho (colored bar)AI lead score 0-100
3.4Import button → CSV uploadCreated/Skipped/Errors summary, duplicates auto-skip
3.5Distribute button → strategy choose → RunUnassigned leads RMs ko bant jayein + RMs ko notify
3.6Export ▾ → CSV/PDFFile download
Why CSV Import? Purana data ya offline events ke leads bulk me daalne padte hain — ek-ek manually nahi. Dedup isliye taake same lead do baar na aaye.
Why Distribution? 100 leads aaye, kis RM ko de? Manually baantna slow + unfair. Round-robin = barabar, Performance = achhe RM ko zyada (kyunki wo zyada convert karega → zyada revenue), Manual = specific RM ko. Auto-notify taake RM turant kaam shuru kare.

End-to-End Scenarios — Pura flow ek saath

Real business jaise — connected steps

🎯 Scenario A — Lead se Paisa tak (poora sales cycle)

  1. admin: Leads → Import CSV (ya New) → naye leads aaye
  2. Leads → Distribute → RMs me baant → RM ko notify
  3. rm.b login: My Workspace me apne leads dikhe → ek lead pe call log karo
  4. Lead serious → Opportunity banao (expected revenue) → stage won
  5. Customer ban gaya → Customer 360 me Add Revenue
  6. finance: Finance Dashboard me revenue + profit update
  7. admin: Incentives → Auto-Calculate → RM ko incentive → payroll me flow

→ Ye dikhata hai sab modules connected hain: lead → deal → customer → revenue → incentive → payroll.

🏖️ Scenario B — Leave ka pura cycle

  1. rm.b: My Leaves → Apply → pending
  2. tl: 🔔 notification → Leaves → Approve (sirf apni team)
  3. rm.b: 🔔 "approved" → status updated

⏰ Scenario C — Din ka kaam (attendance + activity)

  1. rm.b: My Attendance → Check In
  2. Din bhar kaam — calls/notes log karo → counters auto badhein
  3. Sham: Check Out → working hours auto
  4. hr: HR Dashboard me present count + activity dikhe

🔐 Scenario D — Admin permission change (live)

  1. admin: Permission Matrix → Sales Manager → Revenue "Edit" ON
  2. smgr login → Revenue → ab Edit button aa gaya
  3. Wapas admin → Business Access → rm.a ko 1 aur business do
  4. rm.a → ab wo business bhi dikhe

Agar ye 4 scenarios chal gaye → pura CRM connected aur working hai. 🎉
