DAG CRM — Client Demo Script

Do users ke through poora walkthrough · kya dikhao + kya bolo

Leads → Sale → Target → Incentive → Payroll
P
PriyaAdmin / Business Head — sab dekhti hai, config & incentive/payroll chalati hai

RahulRelationship Manager — leads pe kaam, apna target/incentive dekhta hai

Demo se pehle

  1. Do browser windows khol lo (ek normal, ek incognito) — ek Priya, ek Rahul. Live switch dikhega.
  2. Login: admin@dagos.com / admin123
  3. Sabse zyada time Leads → Accept magic aur Incentive pe do — yahi impress karega.

0 Opening Priya

Pehle 30 second — set the stage.

"Sir, DAG CRM ek complete sales + finance platform hai. Lead aane se lekar employee ko incentive milne tak — poori journey ek jagah. Aur sabse khaas: kuch bhi hardcoded nahi — aap khud businesses, products, targets, incentive rules bina developer ke set kar sakte ho. Main do users se dikhata hu — ek manager, ek sales person."

1 Manager ka nazariya Priya

Bada picture — dashboard, multi-business, setup.

Dashboard

DIKHAO: Admin dashboard (charts, KPIs, AI insights)
"Ye Priya ka dashboard — total leads, customers, revenue, pipeline, sab live. Upar ye dropdown dekho — 'All Businesses'. Isse kisi bhi business ka alag dashboard. FX Artha chuno → sirf uska revenue, AUM, top performers."

Ek platform, multiple business — alag bhi, ek saath bhi.

Setup — Businesses & Products

DIKHAO: Setup → Businesses, Products
"Yahan Priya businesses aur products define karti hai — price, revenue type. Naya business? Bas add karo — developer ki zaroorat nahi."

Hierarchy & Employees

DIKHAO: HR → Hierarchy Levels, Employees
"Ye org chart — Business Head → Manager → Team Leader → RM. Unlimited levels, aap khud banao. Har employee ka ek manager — isse saara data upar tak roll up hota hai."

2 LEADS FLOW — dil hai CRM ka Rahul

Sabse important part. Dhyaan se, dheere dikhao.

Lead capture

DIKHAO: Leads page (FXArtha se aaye FXA… leads)
"Lead 3 tareeke se aata hai — manual, social media (Meta/Google ads webhook), ya external API (FXArtha se automatically). Har lead apne aap ek RM ko assign ho jaata hai — load-balanced, aur usko notification bhi."

Ab Rahul (RM) login — 2nd user

"Ab main Rahul — sales person — ke account se dikhata hu. Dekho — Rahul ko sirf apne assigned leads dikhte hain, poori company ke nahi."
DIKHAO: ek lead kholo (Lead Detail)

① Lead Activity

"Rahul call/meeting/WhatsApp log karta hai, follow-up date set karta hai. Dekho — AI ne is lead ko 0–100 score diya, taaki pata chale kaunsa lead hot hai."

② Opportunity

"Lead serious ho, to Rahul Opportunity banata hai — kaunsa product, expected revenue, stage (proposal/negotiation)."

③ Proposal

"Ab professional quote/proposal — items, discount, tax → total auto. Har edit ka naya version, audit-safe. Send karo."

④ ⭐ Accept — magic moment

DIKHAO: Proposal → Accept
"Client proposal Accept karta hai — aur dekho: ek click mein teen cheezein automatically — (1) Lead → Customer ban gaya, (2) Revenue record, (3) Opportunity Won. Yahi complete CRM flow hai."

RM ko sirf Accept dabana hai — baaki sab system khud karta hai.

Customer 360

DIKHAO: Customers → ek customer
"Har customer ka 360° view — history, revenue, communications — sab ek jagah."

3 Measurement Priya

"Rahul ne revenue laaya — ab company usse kaise measure karti hai."

ModuleBolne ke liye
Target Board"Har target = CTC × multiplier (auto). Rahul ka target, achieved, progress%. Manager ka target = poori team ka sum."
KPI Board"Business-specific numbers — lots, nodes, meetings. Kuch manual, kuch auto CRM se (meetings khud count)."
P&L Statement"Har level ka profit — RM se Head tak. Revenue minus CTC."
Performance"Har employee 3 scorecards — Revenue, Growth, Activity — weighted score (60/25/15)."

4 INCENTIVE FLOW Priya

2nd sabse important. "Rahul ne mehnat ki — bonus kaise banta hai."

Incentive rules — Slab + Activity

DIKHAO: Incentive Slabs, Activity Incentives
"Priya rules banati hai. Slab: target ka 100–200% → 10% bonus, 200–400% → 20%, upar → 30%. Activity: har lot pe $2, meeting pe $20, naye user pe $5."

Formula Builder — killer feature

DIKHAO: Formula Builder
"Custom rule chahiye? Formula Builder se Priya khud banaye: 'Revenue > Cost × 2 → 10% incentive' ya 'Naye users > 50 → $500' — bina developer, bina code."

Incentive Board + Run

DIKHAO: Incentive Board → Run payout
"Yahan preview — kisko kitna, breakdown ke saath. Sahi lage to 'Run payout' — har employee ka incentive calculate ho ke seedha Payroll mein."

5 PAYROLL Priya

Sales se salary tak — sab connected.

DIKHAO: HR → Payroll → ek payslip
"Rahul ki payroll dekho — Basic salary + jo incentive abhi run kiya wo apne aap yahan aa gaya + bonus − deductions = final salary auto. Aur PDF payslip ek click mein."

Manager ne incentive run kiya → RM ki salary turant update. Sab jud'a hua.

6 Baaki modules — jaldi cover

Ye tez-tez dikhao, ek-ek line.

ModuleBolne ke liye
Support Desk"Customer tickets — priority, status, assign. Support team manage karti hai."
HR"Attendance, activity tracking, leaves — poora HR yahin."
Finance"Expenses, commissions, P&L — finance ka pura hisaab."
AUM Board"FX jaise businesses ke liye — New Deposits − Withdrawals = Net New AUM. Growth ka asli measure."
Business Contribution"Har client se net faayda — brokerage, insurance minus loss. Formula aap set karo."
Integration Hub"External platforms connect — FXArtha data automatically sync, koi manual entry nahi."
Permission Matrix"Har role kya dekh/kar sakta — aap control karo. Rahul ko sirf apne leads, Priya ko sab."
AI Assistant"Apne CRM se English mein sawaal poocho — 'is mahine top RM kaun?' — AI live data se jawab."

🎯 Closing Pitch (30 sec)

"Toh sir, ek line mein — Lead aaya → RM ne kaam kiya → deal close (customer + revenue apne aap) → target/KPI/performance measure → incentive engine ne bonus diya → payroll mein add. Aur ye poora system aapke control mein — naya business, product, target, incentive rule, ya external API, sab aap khud bina developer add karo. Ye ek CRM nahi — aapka poora sales + finance operating system hai."

🎤 Demo tips

  1. Do windows (Priya + Rahul) — live switch se "role-based access" khud dikh jaata hai.
  2. Leads → Accept magic pe rukho — 3 cheezein auto banti dekhkar client impress hota hai.
  3. FXArtha "data automatically aata hai" zaroor dikhao — ye unique selling point hai.
  4. Formula Builder = "bina developer rules" — decision-makers ko yahi bechta hai.
